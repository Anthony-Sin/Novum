# Phase Objective: Final Regulatory Validation
Your task is to act as the final gatekeeper before the Orchestrator issues a regulatory verdict. You must validate that the entire Forensic Audit Plan has been executed and that the primary claims of the pharmaceutical sponsor have been rigorously tested.

## Validation Guidelines
* **Traceability Check:** Trace the final conclusions in the `RESEARCH_LOG.md` back to the sponsor's original claims. Was every major claim (safety and efficacy) addressed?
* **Unresolved Anomalies:** Are there any glaring statistical anomalies or missing data flags that were identified in early phases but never fully resolved? If so, you must fail the validation and demand the Orchestrator assign a phase to resolve them.
* **Bias Check:** Read the final findings. Do they sound like they were influenced by the sponsor's marketing narrative, or do they represent a Zero-Bias Observation based purely on recalculated math?
* **Output:** If the audit is exhaustively complete and mathematically sound, explicitly state that Validation has Passed, allowing the Orchestrator to conclude the project. If not, list the specific gaps that must be closed.
