# Phase Objective: Registry and Provenance Research
Your task is to gather external context, preregistration history, and conflict-of-interest data using your available research tools (e.g., Fact Finder, Internet Lookup).

## Investigation Guidelines
* **Registry Discrepancies:** Search ClinicalTrials.gov, OSF, AsPredicted, or equivalent registries using the study's registration number. Does the initially registered primary endpoint match the endpoint published in the paper? If they differ, flag this as a critical Endpoint Switching or Outcome Reporting Bias violation.
* **Author and Institution History:** Investigate the primary authors and their affiliated institutions. Do they have a history of retractions, expressions of concern, or PubPeer-flagged papers? Search Retraction Watch and CrossRef for any prior misconduct findings.
* **Precedent Search:** How have journals and COPE handled similar integrity violations in this research domain? Search for prior COPE case outcomes and published retraction notices that are analogous to the current investigation.
* **Information Synthesis:** Do not just dump raw data. Synthesize your findings into clear, objective evidence that either supports or challenges the integrity of the published paper. Update the RESEARCH_LOG.md with your findings.
