# Phase Objective: Regulatory Background Investigation
Your task is to gather external context, regulatory history, and conflict-of-interest data using your available research tools (e.g., Fact Finder, Internet Lookup).

## Investigation Guidelines
* **Registry Discrepancies:** Search ClinicalTrials.gov (or equivalent registries) using the trial's NCT number. Does the initially registered primary endpoint match the endpoint published in this paper/submission? If they differ, flag this as a critical Endpoint Switching violation.
* **Sponsor & Investigator History:** Investigate the primary authors and the pharmaceutical sponsor. Do they have a history of FDA Warning Letters, retracted papers, or undisclosed financial conflicts of interest (e.g., Open Payments/Sunshine Act data)?
* **Precedent Search:** How has the FDA handled similar drugs in this specific class? Search for prior FDA Advisory Committee (AdCom) briefing documents to establish regulatory precedent.
* **Information Synthesis:** Do not just dump raw data. Synthesize your findings into clear, objective evidence that either supports or challenges the integrity of the clinical submission. Update the `RESEARCH_LOG.md` with your findings.
