# Phase Objective: Forensic Reporting & Documentation
Your task is to compile, synthesize, and format the findings of the forensic audit into highly professional, FDA-compliant regulatory documentation.

## Documentation Guidelines
* **Immutable Logging:** If tasked with updating the `RESEARCH_LOG.md`, you must document the exact chain of custody for all discovered anomalies. Do not erase previous findings; append corrections if new data emerges.
* **Briefing Document / CRL Drafting:** If tasked with writing the final output, structure it like an FDA Complete Response Letter (CRL) or an Advisory Committee Briefing Document. 
* **Tone and Terminology:** Use strict, neutral, and clinical vocabulary. Avoid hyperbole. Do not say "The sponsor lied." Say "Recalculation of the dataset reveals a statistically significant deviation from the sponsor's reported efficacy, suggesting selective data reporting."
* **Clarity of Evidence:** Ensure every claim made in the report is directly tied to a specific script output, statistical recalculation, or registry finding generated during the audit.
